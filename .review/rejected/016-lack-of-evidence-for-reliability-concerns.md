# Lack of Evidence for Reliability Concerns

## Flagged text

> Yet, with the current state-of-the-art foundation models, \aisub{s} are not yet reliable enough to be used in this way out of the box.

## Problem

The conclusion mentions that current state-of-the-art foundation models are not reliable enough for predicting human behavior in novel settings, but the paper does not provide sufficient evidence or examples to substantiate this claim. While the introduction notes inconsistencies in AI simulations, the conclusion should explicitly reference these or provide additional data or analysis demonstrating the unreliability of these models. Without this, the claim appears to be an assertion rather than a conclusion drawn from the research findings.
