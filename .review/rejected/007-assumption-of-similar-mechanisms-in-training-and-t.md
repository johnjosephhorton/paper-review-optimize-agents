# Assumption of Similar Mechanisms in Training and Target Settings

## Flagged text

> Identify distinct samples of human-generated data that are presumably generated by the same mechanisms as the novel target setting(s) of interest.

## Problem

The paper assumes that the human-generated data samples used for training and testing are generated by the same mechanisms as the novel target settings. This is a critical assumption for the validity of the identification strategy, as any differences in underlying mechanisms could lead to biased predictions. To address this, the paper should provide evidence or a rationale for why these mechanisms are expected to be similar across different settings, or discuss the potential impact of any discrepancies.
