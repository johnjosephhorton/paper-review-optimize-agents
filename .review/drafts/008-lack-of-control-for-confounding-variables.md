# Lack of Control for Confounding Variables

## Flagged text

> Apply the optimized persona{s} to the testing data and evaluate their performance relative to the baseline LLM.

## Problem

The evaluation of optimized persona{s} against a baseline LLM does not mention controlling for potential confounding variables that might influence the performance outcomes. Without controlling for these, it is difficult to attribute differences in predictive performance solely to the optimization of persona{s}. The study should include a discussion of potential confounders and describe how they are controlled for in the analysis, or acknowledge the limitations this imposes on causal interpretation.
