# Acknowledgment of Inconsistencies without Addressing Alternative Explanations

## Flagged text

> Yet within this literature, others find settings where the very same models are poor human proxies. This inconsistency poses a challenge for AI simulations as robust and credible predictive models---particularly in settings where no prior human data exists.

## Problem

The paper acknowledges that there are settings where LLMs fail as human proxies but does not adequately explore or address alternative explanations for these inconsistencies. A more robust argument would involve discussing potential reasons for these failures and considering whether the proposed method could address them. Additional analysis or evidence showing how the proposed approach might overcome these inconsistencies would strengthen the paper's claims about the reliability of AI simulations.
