# Incomplete Method Description

## Flagged text

> A finite set of unique candidate natural lang

## Problem

The description of the selection method is incomplete, which may hinder the reproducibility of the study. Without a full understanding of how candidate personas are selected or mixed, other researchers may struggle to replicate the findings. The authors should provide a complete and detailed explanation of the selection method, including any criteria or algorithms used for selecting or mixing personas.
