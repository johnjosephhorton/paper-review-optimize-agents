# Generalization Across Multiple Related Settings

## Flagged text

> Our approach provides a disciplined “trial run” to confirm whether a given sample of aisub{s} can reliably predict human behavior across multiple related settings.

## Problem

The paper claims that the approach can confirm the reliability of predictions across multiple related settings, but it does not specify how 'related' these settings need to be or what criteria are used to determine this. This is crucial for assessing the external validity of the findings. The paper should clarify the criteria for determining the relatedness of settings and provide examples or evidence showing successful generalization to substantiate this claim.
